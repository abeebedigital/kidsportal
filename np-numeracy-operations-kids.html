🏯 Place Value — What's Each Digit Worth?

In the Number Dojo, the FIRST secret is Place Value! 🏯

Every digit in a number has a position, and that position tells you its VALUE!

Look at the number 3,472:

🔹 3 is in the Thousands place → worth 3,000

🔹 4 is in the Hundreds place → worth 400

🔹 7 is in the Tens place → worth 70

🔹 2 is in the Ones place → worth 2

So 3,472 = 3,000 + 400 + 70 + 2

This is called expanded form! 💪

🏯
Place Value Chart
🔴 Thousands = × 1,000
🟠 Hundreds = × 100
🟡 Tens = × 10
🟢 Ones = × 1

🏯 Comparing & Rounding Numbers!

A ninja always knows which number is bigger or smaller! 🐊

We use these special symbols:

🔹 > means greater than (the big mouth eats the bigger number!)

🔹 < means less than

🔹 = means equal to

Example: 4,526 > 3,981 ✅

Rounding makes numbers simpler! Look at the digit to the RIGHT of the one you're rounding:

🔹 If it's 0–4 → round DOWN

🔹 If it's 5–9 → round UP

Example: Round 367 to the nearest hundred → look at the 6 (tens) → 6 ≥ 5 → round UP → 400! 🎯

🐊
The Hungry Crocodile!
🐊 Mouth opens to the BIGGER number
> 52 > 38 (52 is bigger)
< 15 < 42 (15 is smaller)
= 25 = 25 (they match!)
💡 Numi's Rounding Trick!
Think of a hill! Numbers 1–4 roll BACK down (round down). Numbers 5–9 roll OVER the top (round up)! 🏔️ So 73 rounded to the nearest ten? The 3 rolls back → 70. And 78? The 8 rolls over → 80!

⚔️ Subtraction & Word Problems!

Subtraction is the REVERSE ninja move! Take away, find the difference, or find what's missing! ⚔️

Strategy 1: Count Back

🔹 72 − 5 → count back: 71, 70, 69, 68, 67 ✅

Strategy 2: Jump Back

🔹 85 − 32 → jump back 30: 55, then − 2 = 53

Strategy 3: Add Up (Shopkeeper's Method!)

🔹 "What's 100 − 67?" → start at 67, add up to 100: 67 + 3 = 70, + 30 = 100 → answer is 33!

Word Problem Tip: Look for clue words!

➕ Add: altogether, total, in all, combined, sum

➖ Subtract: left over, remaining, difference, fewer, less than

⚔️
Clue Words!
➕ "altogether" = ADD
➕ "how many in total" = ADD
➖ "how many left" = SUBTRACT
➖ "difference between" = SUBTRACT
📖 Maths Tip!
Challenge questions love word problems! Always read the question TWICE, circle the clue words, and decide if you need to add or subtract BEFORE you start calculating! 🎯

🌀 Multiplication — Times Tables Power!

Multiplication is just FAST adding — groups of the same number! 🌀

3 × 4 means "3 groups of 4" = 4 + 4 + 4 = 12

Numi's times table tricks:

🔹 ×2 = just double it! (7 × 2 = 14)

🔹 ×5 = count by 5s: 5, 10, 15, 20...

🔹 ×10 = add a zero! (6 × 10 = 60)

🔹 ×9 = the finger trick! Hold up 10 fingers. Put down finger #4 for 9 × 4. Count: 3 fingers left, gap, 6 fingers right = 36! 🖐️

🔹 ×4 = double then double again! (6 × 4 = 6×2=12, then 12×2 = 24)

Remember: order doesn't matter! 3 × 7 = 7 × 3 = 21

🌀 Division — Sharing & Grouping!

Division is the opposite of multiplication! It means sharing equally or making groups. ÷

Sharing: 12 ÷ 3 = "Share 12 among 3 people" → each gets 4

Grouping: 12 ÷ 3 = "How many groups of 3 in 12?" → 4 groups

The ninja shortcut? Use your times tables BACKWARDS! 🔄

🔹 24 ÷ 6 = ? → Think: "6 × ? = 24" → 6 × 4 = 24 → answer is 4!

🔹 45 ÷ 9 = ? → Think: "9 × ? = 45" → 9 × 5 = 45 → answer is 5!

Remainders: Sometimes it doesn't divide evenly!

🔹 14 ÷ 3 = 4 remainder 2 (because 3 × 4 = 12, and 14 − 12 = 2 left over)

÷
Division is Backwards ×!
🔄 Division = reverse multiplication
🤝 Sharing = equal parts for each
📦 Grouping = how many groups?
🧩 Remainder = what's left over
💡 Numi's Division Trick!
If you know your times tables, you ALREADY know division! Just flip the question around. "35 ÷ 7 = ?" becomes "7 × ? = 35". The answer is 5! Times tables are the KEY to division mastery! 🔑

💰 Adding Money & Giving Change!

To add money, start with the biggest and count on! 💰

Example: $2 + $1 + 50c + 20c + 5c

→ $2 ... $3 ... $3.50 ... $3.70 ... $3.75 ✅

Giving Change — use the "count up" method!

You buy something for $3.40 and pay with $5.00.

🔹 Start at $3.40

🔹 +10c = $3.50

🔹 +50c = $4.00

🔹 +$1 = $5.00 ✅

Change = 10c + 50c + $1 = $1.60!

Top tip: Always write money with 2 decimal places! $3.40 not $3.4 ✍️

🛒
Shopping Ninja!
💡 Add biggest coins/notes first
🔢 Change = paid − cost
⬆️ "Count up" for easy change
✍️ Always 2 decimal places!
