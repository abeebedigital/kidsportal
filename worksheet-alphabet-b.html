Letter B

The uppercase letter is B. The lowercase letter is b. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase B

Draw one long line down first. Then make the top bump. Last, make the bottom bump.

Lowercase b

Draw the tall line down first. Then go back up a little and make the round belly on the right.
