Letter O

The uppercase letter is O. The lowercase letter is o. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🍊 Orange O is for orange
🦉 Owl O is for owl
🐙 Octopus O is for octopus
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase O

Start at the top and go all the way around to make a big O.

Lowercase o

Start at the top and go around to make a round o.
