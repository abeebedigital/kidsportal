Letter H

The uppercase letter is H. The lowercase letter is h. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

👒 Hat H is for hat
🐔 Hen H is for hen
🏠 House H is for house
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase H

Draw the left line down, the right line down, then add the middle line.

Lowercase h

Draw the tall line down first, then draw the hump to the right.
