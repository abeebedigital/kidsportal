Letter K

The uppercase letter is K. The lowercase letter is k. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase K

Draw one long line down. Then add the top diagonal and the bottom diagonal.

Lowercase k

Draw the tall line down first. Then add the top slant and the lower slant.
