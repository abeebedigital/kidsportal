Letter R

The uppercase letter is R. The lowercase letter is r. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🐰 Rabbit R is for rabbit
🌈 Rainbow R is for rainbow
🤖 Robot R is for robot
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase R

Draw one line down, make the top round part, then add the diagonal leg.

Lowercase r

Draw the line down first, then add the small arm at the top.
