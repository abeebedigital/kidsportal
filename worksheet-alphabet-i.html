Letter I

The uppercase letter is I. The lowercase letter is i. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase I

Draw the top line, then the tall middle line, then the bottom line.

Lowercase i

Draw the short line down first, then add the dot at the top.
