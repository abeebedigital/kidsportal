Meet Leo
A boy who loved helping people in small ways.

In a small village lived Leo, a cheerful boy with muddy boots and a kind smile.

He carried groceries for elders, shared lunch with classmates, and helped neighbors without being asked.

Leo thought these were tiny things, but kindness was already planting something special.

Big idea
Small kind actions are never small to the person who receives them.

Leo's First Seed

🌱

Kind reminder

A Magical Garden
Seeds appeared every time Leo chose kindness.

Something magical happened: every time Leo helped someone, a glowing seed appeared in his garden.

One seed for sharing, one for helping, one for comforting someone who felt sad.

Soon, tiny sprouts poked through the soil and shimmered in the evening light.

The Garden of Good Deeds
Leo's kindness became fruit, flowers, and shelter.

By the end of the season, Leo's garden became extraordinary.

Fruit trees bent with food, giant leaves made cool shade, and bright flowers lit every corner.

People in the village loved passing by, because the garden felt warm and hopeful.

Leo still said, “I only did little things.”

Kindness Multiplies

Power sentence
Kindness does not stay small. It multiplies.

The Great Storm
Wind and rain damaged homes across the village.

One night, dark clouds roared over the village.

Heavy rain flooded streets, and strong winds damaged homes and fences.

Families were wet, cold, and worried. They needed food, cover, and safe space.

Kindness Saved Everyone
Leo's garden gave shelter, food, and strength.

Leo ran to his garden and called, “Come here, everyone!”

Giant leaves sheltered families from rain. Fruit from the kindness trees fed hungry children.

Strong branches were used to repair broken roofs and fences.

The village stayed safe because Leo's kindness had been growing all along.

Important lesson
What you give to others today can become help for many tomorrow.
