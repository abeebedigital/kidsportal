Letter Y

The uppercase letter is Y. The lowercase letter is y. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase Y

Draw the left slant, the right slant to the middle, then the long line down.

Lowercase y

Make the top part first, then take the tail down below the line.
