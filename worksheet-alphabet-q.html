Letter Q

The uppercase letter is Q. The lowercase letter is q. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase Q

Make a big O first, then add the small tail line.

Lowercase q

Make the round part first, then draw the tail down on the right.
