Meet Luma
A tiny star with a big heart and a quiet voice.

High above the sea lived a tiny star named Luma.

Bigger stars sparkled proudly, but Luma stayed behind clouds and whispered, “I am too small to matter.”

Every night she watched the sky shine brightly, yet she believed her own light was too little to help anyone.

Big idea
Sometimes we hide our gifts because we compare ourselves to others.

Luma's Worry

☁️✨☁️
"What if I am too small?"
Kind reminder
The size of your light does not decide its value.

A Dark, Windy Night
Clouds covered the moon, and the sea grew wild.

One stormy evening, thick clouds covered the moon.

Below, a sailor in a small boat lost his way among restless waves.

He looked up and called, “Please... just one star. Show me home.”

Luma heard him. Her tiny heart beat fast.

One Brave Decision
Luma chose courage over fear.

Luma trembled behind the clouds, but she remembered the sailor's voice.

She took a deep breath, peeked out, and let her light shine.

Her glow was not loud. It was not grand. But it was steady.

Sometimes courage means doing one small right thing, even while afraid.

Small but Steady

✨
A tiny light can still lead.
Power sentence

Following the Light
The sailor fixed his eyes on Luma's glow.

The sailor saw Luma's glow and pointed his boat toward it.

Minute by minute, wave by wave, he moved in the right direction.

At last, the shoreline appeared. He was safe.

Luma watched in wonder. Her small light had made a real difference.

Home At Last
Gratitude reached all the way to the sky.

When the sailor reached home, he looked up and whispered, “Thank you, little star.”

Luma glowed a little brighter.

For the first time, she believed the truth: being small does not mean being useless.
