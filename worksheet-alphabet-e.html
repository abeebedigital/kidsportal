Letter E

The uppercase letter is E. The lowercase letter is e. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase E

Draw one long line down. Then add the top, middle, and bottom lines.

Lowercase e

Start in the middle, go across, and curve around to make e.
