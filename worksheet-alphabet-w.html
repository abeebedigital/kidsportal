Letter W

The uppercase letter is W. The lowercase letter is w. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🐋 Whale W is for whale
⌚ Watch W is for watch
🍉 Watermelon W is for watermelon
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase W

Go down, up, down, and up again to make the W.

Lowercase w

Go down, up, down, and up again to make the w.
