Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase D

Draw one long line down first. Then make one big curve for the round side.

Lowercase d

Make the round circle first, then draw the tall line down on the right.
