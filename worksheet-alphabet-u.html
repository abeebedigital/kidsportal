Letter U

The uppercase letter is U. The lowercase letter is u. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase U

Go down one side, curve around the bottom, and come up the other side.

Lowercase u

Go down, curve around, and come up. Then finish the little tail.
