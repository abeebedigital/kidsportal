Letter M

The uppercase letter is M. The lowercase letter is m. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🌙 Moon M is for moon
🐵 Monkey M is for monkey
🥭 Mango M is for mango
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase M

Go down, back up, down again, then up and down to finish the M.

Lowercase m

Draw the line down first, then make one hump, then another hump.
