The Cloud Who Refused to Rain
A Story About Sharing and Joy
☁️ 💧 🌼 🐇 🌈

Cloudy discovers that giving rain to others brings more happiness than keeping it all. · For ages 5–10

Page 2
☁️
Meet Cloudy
A giant cloud who loved staying full and fluffy.

High above the valley floated Cloudy, a big soft cloud who loved looking huge in the sky.

She would proudly say, "Look how puffy I am. I do not want to lose a single drop."

So day after day, she held her rain tightly and never let it fall.

Big idea
Keeping everything for ourselves can feel good at first, but it often hurts others.

Cloudy's Choice

☁️
"I want to stay big forever!"
Kind reminder
What we share can become life for someone else.
Page 3
🌼
A Dry Village
Without rain, flowers wilted and animals got thirsty.

Soon, the garden petals curled, the grass turned pale, and ponds began to shrink.

Rabbits, birds, and deer searched for water all day.

They looked up and whispered, "Please, Cloudy, we need rain."

What Happened Next

1
No rain fell from the sky.
2
Plants and ponds dried out.
3
The village became worried.
Quick Check
Why were the flowers and animals suffering?
Page 4
💭
A Hard Truth
Cloudy saw the damage and felt a tug in her heart.

A gust of wind showed Cloudy cracked soil, tired faces, and silent fields.

For the first time, she wondered if staying full was really worth it.

She realized that her drops were not just water. They were hope.

Turning Point

💧
Small drops can make a big difference.
Power sentence
Giving does not make you smaller. It makes your world bigger.
Page 5
🌧️
The Rain of Kindness
Cloudy finally opened her heart and poured rain.

Cloudy took a deep breath and let her first drop fall.

Then came a gentle, steady rain that soaked the thirsty land.

Children laughed, animals danced, and fields drank gratefully.

How Sharing Helped

1
Rain filled empty ponds.
2
Plants stood up again.
3
Life returned to the valley.
Remember
What we release with love often returns as joy.

A Happier Cloud
Cloudy discovered that sharing felt better than holding on.

As sunlight returned, a rainbow stretched across the sky.

Cloudy looked smaller than before, but she felt lighter and happier than ever.

She smiled and said, "Sharing my rain made everyone bloom, including me."

Important lesson
Sharing what we have can fill many hearts, including our own.
