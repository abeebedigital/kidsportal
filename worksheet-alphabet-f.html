Letter F

The uppercase letter is F. The lowercase letter is f. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🐟 Fish F is for fish
🐸 Frog F is for frog
🌼 Flower F is for flower
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase F

Draw one long line down. Then add the top line and the middle line.

Lowercase f

Draw the tall line with a curve first, then add the small middle line.
