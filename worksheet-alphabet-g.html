Letter G

The uppercase letter is G. The lowercase letter is g. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🐐 Goat G is for goat
🍇 Grapes G is for grapes
🎁 Gift G is for gift
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase G

Make a big curved G first, then draw the short line in.

Lowercase g

Make the round part first, then take the tail down and curve it under.
