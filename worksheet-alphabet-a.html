Letter A

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase A

Start at the bottom left, go up to the top, come down the other side, then draw the middle line.

Lowercase a

Make a round circle first, then draw the short line down on the right side.
