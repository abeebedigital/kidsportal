Letter S

The uppercase letter is S. The lowercase letter is s. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🐍 Snake S is for snake

Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase S

Curve one way at the top, then curve the other way at the bottom.

Lowercase s

Curve one way at the top, then curve back the other way at the bottom.
