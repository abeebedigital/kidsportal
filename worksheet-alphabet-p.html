Letter P

The uppercase letter is P. The lowercase letter is p. Say the letter name, then say the sound you hear at the start of the words below.

🐷 Pig P is for pig
🖊️ Pen P is for pen
🍕 Pizza P is for pizza
Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase P

Draw one long line down first. Then make the top round part.

Lowercase p

Draw the tall line down first, then make the round belly on the right.
