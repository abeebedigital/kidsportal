Page 2 · Trace and write

Trace the letter

Use your finger first. Then trace with a pencil. Last, try writing the letter on your own.

Uppercase N

Draw the left line down, the diagonal across, then the right line up.

Lowercase n

Draw the line down first, then make one hump to the right.
